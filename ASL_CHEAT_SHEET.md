# 🖐️ ASL Cheat Sheet (Geometric Perfection)

To get the best results with this AI, please follow these specific gesture guides. The system uses **3D Geometry** to detect finger states.

---

| Letter | Gesture Description | Visual Tip |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **A** | **Fist (Side Thumb)** | Make a fist, keep thumb on the very side. |
| **B** | **Open Palm** | All fingers straight up and touching each other. |
| **D** | **The 'Pointer'** | Index finger straight up, others in a circle. |
| **F** | **The 'OK' Sign** | Touch Index and Thumb, keep other 3 up. |
| **G** | **Sideways Pointer** | Point your Index finger to the left/right. |
| **H** | **Sideways Two** | Point Index and Middle fingers sideways. |
| **I** | **The Pinky** | Only your pinky finger should be up. |
| **L** | **The 'L' Shape** | Index up, Thumb out (like a real 'L'). |
| **O** | **The Circle** | Touch all 4 fingertips to your thumb tip. |
| **S** | **Fist (Front Thumb)** | Make a fist, keep thumb across the front. |
| **U** | **Two Together** | Index and Middle up and *touching*. |
| **V** | **The 'Peace' Sign** | Index and Middle up and *apart*. |
| **W** | **The 'Three'** | Index, Middle, and Ring fingers up and apart. |
| **Y** | **The 'Hang Loose'** | Only Thumb and Pinky should be out. |
| **5 / Space** | **Wide Open** | Fully spread your hand wide. |

---

## 💡 Troubleshooting Tips

1.  **Stability**: Hold the sign for about **1 second** until the green progress bar at the top fills up.
2.  **Distance**: Keep your hand about **1.5 to 3 feet** away from the camera.
3.  **Background**: Even though this system is robust, a clear view of your hand joints (the digital skeleton) is best.
4.  **Duplicates**: If you want to type the same letter twice (like 'LL'), move your hand away and bring it back.
